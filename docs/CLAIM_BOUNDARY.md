# Claim Boundary

This repository supports a research prototype and seminar paper. It does not establish an operational fire detection system, certified safety system, or deployable naval ship product.

## In Scope

- Public-data-only research.
- Ship-like multi-camera concepts.
- MP4-first emulation of NVR-like inputs.
- Future CPU-only ONNX Runtime inference.
- Manifested data provenance and reproducible local organization.
- Early warning and damage-control support as a research framing.

## Out of Scope

- Real naval ship CCTV.
- Classified ship layouts.
- Live RTSP camera integration.
- Operational damage-control doctrine automation.
- GPU inference requirements.
- Docker or WSL deployment.
- Runtime Torch or Ultralytics dependency.

Any paper or demo should clearly state that the prototype is evaluated on public and synthetic data only.
